How it works

Write, swipe, save.

Mitchmo is built around the feeling of sending a text message. There is no complicated folder screen before you start. Just open the app and write.

1

Enter a memo and tap send to create a blue memo.

2

Swipe left on important memos to turn them green.

3

Tap the check button to save only green memos.

4

Delete temporary blue memos all at once when you are done.
